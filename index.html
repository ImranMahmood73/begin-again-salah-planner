Private Salah Recovery Planner

Begin Again

A gentle, private way to estimate, plan and keep moving forward with make-up salah.

Scholars differ regarding deliberately missed prayers. This planner supports people following qualified guidance to make them up. It does not issue religious rulings.

Build your planning estimate

You do not need perfect records. Start with what you reasonably remember, then adjust later if needed.

This first version is designed for adult Muslim men estimating ordinary missed five-daily prayers. Women’s calculations need a separate pathway because menstruation and postnatal bleeding affect the estimate.

Step 1: Your broad dates

Approximate months are enough. This is only a planning tool.

Step 2: Prayer phases

If your prayer pattern changed over time, add those phases. If you are unsure, keep it simple.

Step 3: Choose an estimate

Balanced is the default. Choose lower or cautious only if that better reflects your situation.

Preview will appear once the dates and prayer phases are entered.

Step 4: Create your plan

Your information stays on this device. No account or cloud database is used.

Your current prayers come first. This plan is only here to help you move forward steadily.
